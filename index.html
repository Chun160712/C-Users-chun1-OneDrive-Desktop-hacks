SMART Studying

Welcome to SMART studying! This website is made to help students motivate themselves in order to succeed in school as well as to help them set up SMART goals (long term goals) with some daily tasks. Every day, you can input the date and the daily tasks you need to accomplish. By the end of the day, YOU will give YOURSELF a rating of how satisfied you are with your work. And, by the end of the week, WE will provide you some feedback so that you can push yourself to focus on school (or other important projects).
